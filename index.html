Fun Learning Project

Flarn

Design & Development

Flarn is a Fun Learning platform where people can sign up and solve simple fun questions. It is built with Spring Boot with a Postgres database and VueJS. It is hosted on Heroku and Netlify. Flarn follows good design patterns and practices.

Users are able to view their timeline using their own activities and stories (activities from users they follow). Users can then attempt problems, view people on the platform and subscribe to other users. Users can then vie the various ranking of other users. People gain points as they solve more problems. Users can also edit their profile by uploading new profile pictures. Different (learners, moderators, admin) have a different views of the user interface.
